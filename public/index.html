Meet the Characters in Marvel Studios'
The Falcon and The Winter Soldier


The Falcon and the Winter Soldier is an upcoming Disney+ Original, the second in a long line of Marvel Studios and Disney+ collaborations. It details the characters Sam Wilson, the Falcon, and Bucky Barnes, The Winter Soldier, and their journey into a world post-Thanos, without their closest friend, Steve Rogers, AKA Captain America. Together, they'll face new foes and adjust to leading lives without a main character to stand behind.
